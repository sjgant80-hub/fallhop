FallHop — Bitchat becomes Konomi

FallHop speaks Bitchat's binary wire format on the outside and Konomi (did:key + 7 rings + F(S⃗)) on the inside. Estate messages ride Jack Dorsey's Bluetooth mesh; Bitchat packets arrive as estate-native messages. Bitchat is treated as a lossy legacy protocol we translate to and from — not a peer we imitate.

Estate face is Konomi-native. Everything Bitchat carries that has no Konomi analog is preserved under bitchat_only so konomiToBitchat can reconstruct the original wire packet byte-for-byte on the return path. That's the honest v1 contract.
